MedZap

App for communication between healthcare professionals and their patients

In the rapidly evolving healthcare sector, the need for efficient communication between healthcare professionals and their clients post-consultation is crucial. Traditional methods often lead to delays, miscommunication, and overlooked emergencies, posing a significant challenge in providing timely and effective care.

MedZap addresses these challenges by integrating AI support and a ticket system management into its digital health platform. This solution enhances the responsiveness of healthcare providers, enabling them to detect emergencies quickly and answer queries more effectively.
